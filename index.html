Isopod cultures available!

We have a select few species of isopods currently culturing in base.
They help with breakdown of organic matter (feeds on dead OM) and take up heavy
metals in the soil. Creates frass compost and helps with aeration of the soil
and thereby reducing mold and improving soil structure. Currently
we can only supply small amounts of these species but will aim to grow more if there's
demand for it. (28/11/2024)
